HOW TO BOOK DAN

  • Use the Contact Page or email to get in touch with some information about you project

  • You will receive a quote and 'spec read' if required

  • Listen and produce while recording is underway via Source Connect or Skype or get a ‘ready to go’ set of ads with different versions to choose from

  • Files will be delivered 'clean' at 128kbs mono unless otherwise specified (wav, aiff available and/or any sample rate or bit depth)

  • Finished audio can be supplied with added compression or any specified voice effects upon request

  • Turnaround time is 24 hours from receiving script - unless otherwise requested
