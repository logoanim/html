Audio + Video

Audio

Video

A combined audio and video solution enables integrated audio and video with the option of playing separate or synchronised multimedia content at any time.

With an integrated solution, not only can you enhance your environment and customer interaction through multiple mediums independently, you can also combine them at any time to deliver powerful multimedia communications.

VIDEO + AUDIO

Our unique software and clever scheduling can play separate audio and video for a period of time and then come together for special content. For example, the audio system can provide enhanced information about the products and services being displayed on the video screens, full TV commercials can play in the break between songs or the video screens can let customers know what song is currently playing.
